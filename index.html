Project Description

Our project aims to improve communication between nurses and patients in a hospital to enable nurses to interact with their patients as much as possible and provide excellent care. This product is a messaging application that allows for quick and straightforward messaging between patients and nurses. The key features are: access to patient records for both patients and nurses, a message inbox, differentiation between inpatient and outpatient messages in a nurseâ€™s inbox, and multiple action options for nurses replying to inpatient messages. The nurses and patients will input their respective credentials to gain access to this application. Our application has few pathways, and is meant to strengthen the connection between nurses and patients, as well as simplify the process of messaging between the two groups in order to convey the most important information.
