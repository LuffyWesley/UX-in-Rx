What you can do with this program:

You can use the converter to switch between 2 sets of currencies (crypto or physical). If you do choose a crypto currency, we will also display a graph showing its price history.

Below are some example currencies you can use for this program:

  • United Arab Emirates = AED
  • United States = USD
  • Taiwan = TWD
  • Kenya = KES
  • Bitcoin = BTC
  • Ethereum = ETH
  • Litecoin = LTC
